How to Play

👆

Tap to Jump

Avoid cows, potholes, and broken autos by jumping.

👈

Swipe to Dodge

Dodge obstacles sideways when a quick turn is needed.

🦆

Duck Under Banners

Some obstacles require crouching to pass safely.

❤️

Health System

Collide with obstacles = lose health. Hit zero = Game Over. One revive per session.

🚀

Speed Increases

Road gets faster as you survive. Difficulty ramps smoothly.
