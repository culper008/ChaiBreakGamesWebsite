Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How do I report a bug?

Email us at hello@chaibreak.games with a detailed description of the bug, your device model, and Android version. Screenshots or videos help!

❓ Can I restore my progress if I reinstall the game?

Yes! Your progress is saved to your Google Play Games account if you're signed in. Just sign in again after reinstalling, and your save will be restored.

❓ Why did I lose my high score?

High scores are saved locally on your device. If you clear app data or uninstall the game, local saves may be lost. Signing into Google Play Games ensures your scores are synced to the cloud.

❓ How do I claim my weekly challenge reward?

Complete the weekly challenge before the deadline, then tap "Claim ₹10 Reward" on the Game Over screen. The payout is sent to your UPI account via PhonePe within 10-30 seconds.

❓ Is Jugaad Racer available in my country?

Jugaad Racer is currently available in India on Google Play Store. International release is planned for future updates.

❓ How can I get early access to new features?

Join our Discord community to be notified about beta testing opportunities and new releases.

❓ What should I do if the app crashes?

Try: (1) Updating to the latest version from Google Play, (2) Clearing app cache (Settings → Apps → Jugaad Racer → Storage → Clear Cache), (3) Reinstalling the app. If issues persist, email us details about when it crashes.
